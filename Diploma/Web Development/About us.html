Our History

In 2019, five friends gather and decided to open a restaurant. In 2020, the 1st restaurants began called TOS Burger has been opened in Kuala Lumpur. Now, as they says, is history. Now, TOS Burger® becoming the diamond in the rough among organization-organizations in Malaysia. Today, TOS Burger having about 3 branches which were Johor, Penang, and Perak. The five persons’ success story will continue with TOS Burger families of employees, franchisees and suppliers as long as well. Their commitment, determination and achievements wont died and must be live with and inherited at TOS Burger® restaurants across the world. As the dark horse fast food and leading QSR brand in Malaysia, we are continue working to provide the best service to our customers with these marvelous moments, well-done services and also TOS Burger’s food.
